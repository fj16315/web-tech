What is Cookpot?

Cookpot is a recipe sharing website for people who love cooking!

about us

We're some uni students who love sharing recipes!

Ever since joining uni cooking has been a new hurdle we've had to overcome. After lending of cookbooks and sharing of handwritten notes we came to the realisation that there must be an easier way.

So we made Cookpot, allowing everyone could share their recipes.
