Get a free fridge!

One of the main things that spurred us on make this was clearing out the fridge. Finding things that could've been cooked and eaten a week ago but wasn't. Thats why we wanted to add a way to keep track of food thats about to go out of date.

By using the virtual fridge our website recommends recipes using ingredients that are about to go out of date in your virtual fridge! Even if you're stopping by our improved ingredients search allows you to use as many ingredients as possible.

Save money and the planet

By focusing on reducing the number of ingredients that go out of date it means you don't have to spend as much on new ones and even reduces the amount of food waste.
